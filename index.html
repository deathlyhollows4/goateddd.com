Unlike quick-fix AI tools, they needed an educational platform that acts as a virtual CS professor—diagnosing code, tracing mastery levels across 20+ DSA topics, and generating practice questions customized to each student's current skill gaps.

ROLE SaaS Product Architecture, Full-Stack Engineering, AI Integration
